Description of This No-rise Pizza Dough recipe

This no-rise pizza dough recipe is quick and merely involves mixing a few basic ingredients and patting the dough into the pan - no need to wait for the dough to rise with this approach.

This recipe is great for those working - who just don't have enough time to be sitting around waiting for the pizza dough to get ready and can instead get ready for eating!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.

    2. To Bake the Dough:

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees C (190 degrees C). Pat and stretch dough onto a large pizza pan. Cover with desired toppings. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
